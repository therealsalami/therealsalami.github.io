Part 1: Basic RNNs

Analysis of the vanilla recurrent architecture. This section covers the core mechanics of state updates $H_t = \phi(X_t W_{xh} + H_{t-1} W_{hh} + b_h)$ and the inherent challenges in learning long-term dependencies.

Implementation and evaluation of Gated Recurrent Units. We explore how the gating mechanism controls the information flow, significantly improving the model's ability to maintain context over extended sequences.
